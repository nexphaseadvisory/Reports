Methodology

Each of the 20 priority markets is scored 1–10 from four factors. The three continuous factors are min-max normalized across the 20 states, then weighted:

  • Avg Infrastructure Density (35%) — mean transmission-infrastructure density of the state's interconnection-queue substations.
  • STRONG Substation Count (25%) — substations rated STRONG for infrastructure density (deep, active queue + high voltage).
  • Weighted Load Growth (25%) — utility retail-sales YoY growth, weighted by load; excludes non-organic reporting outliers.
  • Saturation Tier (15%) — DC build-out maturity (underdeveloped > emerging > saturated — more runway scores higher).
